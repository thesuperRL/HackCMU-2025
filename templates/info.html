About This Project

Why we built Extinguish, and who’s behind it.

Why We Made It

Spotted lanternflies are one of the most damaging invasive pests in the U.S. They threaten farms, forests, and communities. We wanted to create a project that doesn’t just fight them, but also makes participation fun and rewarding.

Extinguish turns pest control into a challenge: take a photo, log the catch, and add it to the community map. Every action counts toward protecting ecosystems — and you earn points, badges, and bragging rights along the way.

Who We Are

We’re a small team of students and builders who came together during HackCMU 2025. Our backgrounds range from coding and design to environmental science. What unites us is the belief that technology + community can solve real-world problems.
